About me

I'm currently a visiting postdoc at IMS, CUHK (until Sep. 25, 2023). I plan to apply for a tenure-track position this year.

I was a postdoc at YMSC (Jan.2021-Jul.2022), and at ENS Paris (Sep.2018 - Sep.2020). I obtained my math PhD at UC Berkeley in the summer of 2018, advised by Vivek Shende, and Richard E.Borcherds.

My research interests include microlocal sheaf theory and Legendrian knots, Hodge theory of character varieties and P=W, and mirror symmetry.
